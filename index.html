Risk Assessment Methodology & Spatial Join Logic

1. Spatial Join & Distance Calculations (Haversine Formula)

To assess facility vulnerability in real-time, the system executes a geographic spatial join between the coordinates of 309 Kedah Healthcare Facilities (from the official KKM Registry) and live coordinates of 244 active JPS Telemetry Stations. Distances are calculated using the Haversine formula to find the shortest path across the Earth's surface:

d = 2R · arcsin(√(sin²(Δlat/2) + cos(lat1) · cos(lat2) · sin²(Δlon/2)))

Where R is the Earth's radius (6,371 km), Δlat and Δlon represent the coordinate differences in radians, and d is the output distance.

2. Risk Level Decision Matrix

The real-time threat level for each facility is computed dynamically by mapping spatial buffers against telemetry thresholds:

Risk Level JPS River Level (WL) Conditions JPS Rainfall (RF) Conditions Historical Hotspot Conditions
HIGH Nearest WL station is in Danger status within 5km, OR in Warning status within 3km. Local rainfall is Very Heavy or ≥ 50 mm/h within a 2km radius. N/A (Historical markers alone do not trigger HIGH risk without live telemetry alerts).
MEDIUM Nearest WL station is in Warning status > 3km (up to 5km), OR in Alert status within 3km with a Rising trend. Local rainfall is Very Heavy/≥ 50 mm/h between 2km and 5km, OR Heavy/≥ 30 mm/h within 3km. N/A (Saves resources for verified active threats).
LOW Nearest WL station is in Alert status (outside 3km or Stable/Falling trend) within 5km. Local rainfall is Heavy > 3km, OR Moderate within 5km. Facility coordinates are within 250 meters of a historical flood hotspot.
NORMAL Nearest WL station has no active alerts or is > 5km away. No active rainfall warnings or heavy rain > 5km away. No historical hotspots within 250m.

3. Verification & Credibility Mechanisms

  • Real-Time API Source: Data is dynamically parsed from the official JPS Public Info Banjir API (latestreadingstrendabc.json), ensuring that values match the official government portal exactly.
  • Direct Audit Trail: Every telemetry station listed in the JPS Telemetry Stations tab and map popups includes a direct verification link to open the official JPS website and audit the reading.
  • Traceable Excel Data: Facility locations are joined using the official KKM registry document Fasiliti Kesihatan Awam Sehingga 31 Disember 2025.xlsx.
  • Timestamped Operations: The dashboard features a prominent live update timestamp at the top of the sidebar showing the exact execution date and time of the spatial join.
